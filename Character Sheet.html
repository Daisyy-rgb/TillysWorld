Character

Tilly is twelve years old and has already learned that the world is not always kind. Raised above a cluttered antique shop in the city Alderrdeen by her grandfather, she has a gift for knowing exactly what something is worth, and exactly what someone can pay. She is sharper than she looks, and kinder than she has any reason to be. She asks for very little in return for either. She loves deeply and without condition, and it has never once occurred to her that this might someday cost her something.
